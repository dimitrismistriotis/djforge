Cookie Policy Document

Introduction:
Our website (the "Website") uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to collect information about your use of the Website. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and our practices regarding user control over cookie settings.

What are cookies?
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (such as your computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit our Website. They help us remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Cookies can be session cookies (which are deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a longer period of time).

Types of cookies used on our Website:
Our Website uses two types of cookies:
1. Session management cookies: These cookies are used to enable you to navigate and interact with our Website. They remember your preferences and login information, and help us provide you with a more personalized experience.
2. Web analytics cookies: These cookies are used to collect information about your use of our Website, such as the pages you visit and the links you click on. This helps us understand how users interact with our Website and make improvements to the user experience. These cookies are not deleted when you close your browser and will remain on your device for a longer period of time.

Control over cookie settings:
While we provide information about the types of cookies used on our Website, we do not offer users the ability to control which specific cookies are being used. This is because certain cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our Website, and disabling them may affect your ability to use certain features and services.

Changes to our Cookie Policy:
We reserve the right to update this Cookie Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy on our Website. Your continued use of our Website after the effective date of any update to this Cookie Policy will constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.
